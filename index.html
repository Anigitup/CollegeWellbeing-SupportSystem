We are with you.

This is a judgment-free space for students to unite against campus ragging and harassment. By speaking up safely here, we can work together to create positive change.

If you're being ragged, you can report incidents anonymously so colleges take action. We're here to provide counseling support, legal advice, campus policy guidance and any other resources you need. You aren't alone. We stand together against ragging, and harassment, and we will make campuses safer for all students.
